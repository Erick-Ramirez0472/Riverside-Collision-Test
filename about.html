About Riverside

Built on Excellence

Since 1988, Riverside Collision Repair has served Utah County with collision repair, maintenance, and a customer experience built on skill, honesty, and careful work.

Waiting Room Photo Add a photo named waiting-room.jpg to this folder and it will appear here.

We began in 1988 as a collision repair shop. Our clients soon began bringing their vehicles to us for second opinions on maintenance quotes from other shops. After years of giving second opinions on quotes, and feeling that in most cases those customers were being overcharged, we added maintenance to our side of the business. We have been offering both collision and maintenance services ever since.

In 2014, we moved to a larger location. We pride ourselves on offering a quality experience, not only with the care we provide for your vehicles, but your personal experience as well. Owner Ron Fox is a staple in the shop. He works directly with clients to understand their concerns and help them understand their options. He also works directly with his team and on the vehicles.

We are proud of the quality of our work. The vehicles that we send out of our shop are our resume and proof that we stand behind our goal of excellence. Our technicians are highly experienced; each has at least ten years of experience before they start at our shop. They are people who are passionate about cars and love being in this industry. That is why, among our loyal customers, we have auto dealers that send us all of their collision work.

Our painter has been with us since the beginning. We use only high-quality, European, water-based paints that perfectly match the original color of each car.

We have a long reputation with high-end cars, too. We are the exclusive maintenance and repair shop for many clients who are collectors. Our customers trust us with their Porsches, Aston Martins, Lamborghinis, and Ferraris, among others.

Owner Ron Fox began his career at an early age, helping his father, who recycled cars. He then worked for a Porsche/Audi dealership in Utah County. He has a special fondness and skill with German cars because so much of his early education on vehicles started with Porsches and Audis. Ron knew he would open his own shop one day, and after 26 years his shop is still going strong.

Our shop is a part of your community. We are a regular sponsor for the UVU annual car show to support students of UVU studying in our field, and we also participate in and support other local car shows.
